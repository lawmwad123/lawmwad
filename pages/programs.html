Our Labs

Our Programs

Four specialized labs driving innovation across engineering disciplines.

01

Software Lab

Our Software Lab develops intelligent systems that power everything from autonomous navigation to educational platforms. We work at the intersection of AI, machine learning, and embedded systems.

  • AI & Machine Learning pipelines
  • Embedded systems programming
  • Signal processing & SDR
  • Computer vision & perception
  • Real-time control algorithms
02

Robotics Lab

From autonomous drones to swarm robotics, our Robotics Lab builds systems that operate in the real world. We design, build, and test robots for navigation, surveillance, delivery, and research.

  • Autonomous drone platforms
  • Ground vehicle navigation
  • Swarm intelligence systems
  • ROS-based development
  • LEGO robotics education
03

Mechanics Lab

The Mechanics Lab focuses on physical systems — from next-gen motor design to advanced manufacturing. We combine traditional engineering principles with cutting-edge fabrication techniques.

  • Electric motor design & optimization
  • 3D printing & rapid prototyping
  • Thermoelectric cooling systems
  • Structural analysis & FEA
  • Electroaerodynamic propulsion
04

Electronics Lab

Our Electronics Lab designs the circuits and sensor systems that bring our projects to life. From PCB design to IMU calibration, we handle the hardware backbone of every project.

  • Custom PCB design & fabrication
  • IMU calibration testbeds
  • Sensor fusion & integration
  • Power systems & battery management
  • RF & antenna design
